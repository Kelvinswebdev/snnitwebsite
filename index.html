DISCLAMER

It has come to the attention of the Social Security and National Insurance Trust (SSNIT) that some unscrupulous persons are calling pensioners under the guise of assisting them to claim “locked up balance of their pension” at a fee. Management of SSNIT wishes to inform the public to disregard such calls as there is no “locked up pension balance” to be collected
